Limitations / Not Investment Advice

Research boundaries

This dashboard is a portfolio evidence view. It does not assign probabilities, forecast market reactions, estimate expected returns, or provide trading or investment recommendations.

Similarity scores and pathway counts are descriptive summaries of coded historical analogues. Evidence notes should be read as qualitative context, not as causal proof.
